Mini MI Lab #1

SPEAKERS TOPIC:

Something about yourself that you:

  • Want to change
  • Need to change
  • Should change
  • Have been thinking about changing
  • But haven't changed yet
  • Pick a behavior you want to change but not your deepest, darkest secret.
LISTENER'S ROLE:

Listen carefully with the goal of understanding the dilemma. Give no advice. You may also ask these three open-ended questions and listen with interest:

  • Why would you want to make this change?
  • How might you go about this change, in order to succeed?
  • What are the best reasons to do it?

Challenge yourself to only reflect.
