Welcome to Training in Motivational Interviewing!

You will progress through a web-based training beginning with a baseline assessment of your therapy skills. Once you have completed the assessment (the VASE-R) you can begin the training modules. The assessment should be completed independently and do not require video recording. The training modules should be completed in an office with recording equipment and a partner.
